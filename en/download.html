APPLICATION DOWNLOAD

I-TUBER LIVE FACE is designed to stream face-tracking data from the iPhone to desktop clients.
Using this app, you can perform facial motion capture in real time on I-TUBER CREATOR software.
Launch the app to connect desktop clients to the PC that shares the same Wi-Fi source.
Enter the client IP address in I-TUBER LIVE FACE app to activate the data feed
