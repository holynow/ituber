How to create contents

  • step 01 Download PC Software

    Download I-TUBER CREATOR PC software

    *Minimum requirements for I-TUBER CREATOR : GTX1060 graphic card.

    Download Now
  • step 02 Download Mobile App

    Download “ituberliveface” App from App Store

    *Currently developing a webcam version. (Not compatible with android)

  • step 03 Choose a Character and Assign Animations

    Choose a character and up to ten animation-motions you want to use, and assign those motions to the numeric keys 0 to 9.

  • step 04 Select a Themed Room and Set Up Items

    Select a themed room where your character will appear.
    You may change the sizes and locations of items in the room if you want.

  • step 05 / 1 Set Up the Facial Capturing IP

    Run the mobile app and connect it to PC software.
    Enter the software facial IP address on the app.

    *PC and mobile must be connected to the same Wi-Fi.
    Facial motion capture doesn’t work on cellular data mode.

  • step 05 / 2

    Check whether the character copies your facial expressions through I-TUBER Live face app.

  • step 06 Live or Record

    Click the ON AIR button and choose live or record button to start creating contents.

    *If you click the ON AIR button again, live-streaming or recording will stop.
